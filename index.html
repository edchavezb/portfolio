About Me

Hello! My name is Edgar and I am a Full-stack Web Developer currently living in Mexico City. I am a curious and motivated professional who loves exploring the opportunities that recent innovations are bringing to our lives. I would love to build something that helps people and communities function more efficiently and sustainably. My favorite activities are API and React development. I have experience working with Javascript, CSS, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, MySQL, Firebase and jQuery, among others.

I successfully completed a full-stack development bootcamp offered by Tecnológico de Monterrey. This six-month course helped me acquire technical skills to build scalable web applications by learning modern frontend and backend technologies. Across three major projects, I worked with a team of fellow developers and learned to use version control software to segment the workload.
